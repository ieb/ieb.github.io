Sailing Weekends, leaving Friday night or Saturday morning, sleeping overnight on board, returning Sunday tea time. For those that have never been, the Boat is Isador a Hallberg Rassy 38 (see sister ship) berthed at Shotley Point Marina . The distanced sailed will be crew experience and weather dependent.

You need to bring, sheet/pillow/duvet/sleeping bag. Some form of waterproof footwear, not Hobnail boots. You will need sunglasses and sun block and perhaps a hat. We will share food/drink costs everything else is on the house. I will be driving from Cambridge and have space for 4 in the car (including myself).

Places are limited, max 4 per weekend, and I cant go on all weekends only a selection.
